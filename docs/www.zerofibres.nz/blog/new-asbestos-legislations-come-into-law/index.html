New Asbestos Legislations come into Law!

Thursday September 06 2018

As of the 4th of April this year it is now mandatory for ALL PCBU's in control of a workplace to have an Asbestos Management Plan if they have presumed or confirmed by lab analysis their building contains Asbestos Containing Materials (ACM'S). Contact Joe if your Asbestos Register and associated compliance documents need reviewing or updating. Make sure your workers and contractors are safe and you are ready if Worksafe call!
